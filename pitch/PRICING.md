### Quick wins — one-time projects ($300–$900 each, ship in 1–2 weeks)

These are the highest leverage per dollar. Each one makes your site more visible to AI search and more useful to visitors.

- **"San Diego Tax Deadlines 2026" cornerstone page** — a single page that catalogs every federal, California, and San Diego County tax deadline with dates, who it applies to, and what to do. AI search engines love this kind of dated, specific, locally-anchored content — it's the most likely thing on your site to get cited by ChatGPT and Google's AI for the next 12 months. **~$600 one-time.**

- **Tax & real estate glossary** — a single page with 60+ terms (1031 exchange, capital gains, depreciation recapture, etc.) each defined in two clear sentences. AI search tools quote definition-style content more than any other format. **~$500 one-time.**

- **Case studies hub** — 4–6 anonymized client stories with specific dollar outcomes ("San Diego real estate investor — $14,200 saved via S-Corp election"). Builds trust with prospects and gives AI tools concrete claims to quote. **~$600 one-time.**

- **Lead magnet pack** — three downloadable PDFs (Tax Deduction Checklist, Real Estate Investor's Tax Guide, Small Business Tax Calendar) wired into the contact form so visitors trade an email for the download. Builds your email list. **~$400 one-time.**
